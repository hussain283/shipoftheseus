Ship of Theseus

Ship of Theseus is a private investment vehicle focused on public and private markets. We seek out businesses that endure — companies that grow, evolve, and adapt without losing themselves.

The ancient paradox from which we take our name asks how identity persists through continuous change. Markets change, businesses change, and portfolios change. Yet the pursuit of sound judgment, continuous learning, and thoughtful risk management remains constant.
